Drawing and Painting Classes

The course is designed to introduce students to drawing and painting, exposing them to various mediums such as oil pastels, pencils, watercolors, and acrylics. All students will be encouraged to express themselves through art, creating their own artwork from observation and imagination. In an hour-long class, they will receive individual instructions and guidance, including drawing principles, blending techniques, color theory, and much more based on their skill level.
